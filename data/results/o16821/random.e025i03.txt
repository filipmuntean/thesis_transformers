 The podcast for Sylvia Earle and Vance McPherson. Because when you become a pilot, you become a permanent citizen.  This podcast is for Virgin Pilots, Virgin Aircraft Owners, and new Virgin Air passengers.  All Virgin Pilots, Virgin Aircraft Owners, and new Virgin Air passengers are welcome to learn about the issues and things you need to know about flying and becoming a passenger.  As an experienced pilot, you'll find out useful how-to's and how to fly tips and tricks along the way.  Read the show notes at http://www.pilotsdaily.com/podcast/ and visit http://www.virginair.com/podcast so you can be informed as we guide you through the journey of becoming a passenger.  Be sure to check out the website (http://www.VirginAir.com) for more about becoming a passenger and be reminded of the importance of growing in the air as a pilot.
